Aim and Scope

International Research Journal of Multidisciplinary Technovation (IRJMT) (ISSN 2582-1040 (Online)) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal published in the English – language, provides an international forum for the publication of Engineering and Technology Researchers. IRJMT is dedicated to publishing clearly written original articles, review articles and short communication in the precinct multidiscipline of Science and Engineering which includes Chemistry, Physics, Biotechnology, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Environmental Engineering (Chemical and Material Science), Mechanical Engineering and General Material Science. It is issued regularly once in two months and open to both research and industry contributions. The core apparition of International Research Journal of Multidisciplinary Technovation (IRJMT) is to spread the innovative knowledge to the society, which paves way to promote Industry contributions, academic and research professionals in the precinct multidiscipline of Engineering and Technology. Its eminence will be on publishing quality papers rapidly and readiness for the researchers worldwide.
