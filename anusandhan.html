Aim and Scope

The primary aim of Anusandhan Journals is to promote research-oriented education and build a strong academic research ecosystem that supports innovation, creativity, critical thinking, and social development. The journals encourage meaningful research contributions that bridge the gap between academics, industry, and society.

Scope of Publication

RNTU Journals publishes high-quality multidisciplinary research contributions across diverse academic and professional domains including Science, Technology, Engineering, Management, Commerce, Humanities, Education, Social Sciences, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Environmental Studies, Innovation, and Emerging Technologies. The journal welcomes original research papers, review articles, case studies, technical reports, analytical studies, conceptual papers, and interdisciplinary research that contribute to academic advancement, industry innovation, and societal development. Through a rigorous peer-review process and open-access publishing model, RNTUJ aims to provide researchers, academicians, scholars, scientists, and students a global platform to share innovative ideas, impactful discoveries, and research-driven solutions for contemporary challenges.
