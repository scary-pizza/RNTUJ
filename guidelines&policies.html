AUTHOR GUIDELINES

INSTRUCTIONS FOR AUTHORS

Welcome to the manuscript submission guidelines of the Asian Research Association (ARA). These guidelines are designed to help authors prepare, format, and submit their research manuscripts in accordance with the journal's publication standards and ethical requirements.

Before submitting your manuscript, we strongly encourage all authors to carefully read and understand the instructions provided in the following sections.

The information presented below covers all essential aspects of manuscript preparation, including submission requirements, document formatting, authorship responsibilities, ethical considerations, title page preparation, manuscript structure, reference formatting, figures and tables, acknowledgements, and reviewer recommendations.

PROCESS

SUBMISSION PROCESS

  • All manuscripts must be submitted electronically through the online submission system.
  • Authors are advised to follow the Author Guideline before preparing the manuscript.
  • We do not accept multiple submissions from the same authors simultaneously.
  • The corresponding author is responsible for communication during review and publication.
MANUSCRIPT PREPARATION

NEW MANUSCRIPTS

Authors can submit their article as a single Word document or PDF for the reviewing process.

DOCUMENT FORMAT REFERENCE

FORMATTING

Abstract (200-350 words), Keywords (4-6), Introduction, Materials & Methods, Figures and Tables with Captions, Results and Discussion, Conclusions, Acknowledgement, Funding Details, Author Contributions, Conflict of Interest, and other Ethics Statements.

Manuscript references should be formatted in IEEE style.
PAGE LAYOUT

TITLE PAGE

The title page is a separate page that comes before the content.

Title

Concise and instructive. Avoid acronyms and formulae wherever possible.

Authors' Names and Affiliations

Corresponding author must be specified to handle contact during the reviewing, publishing, and post-publication processes.

Author's Contact Information

Provide phone number, email address, ORCID ID, and complete postal address.

RNTU JOURNALS

Acknowledgements

Contributors who fail to meet the criteria for authorship should be acknowledged in the acknowledgements section.

If the manuscript was submitted on behalf of a consortium, the names and affiliations of all authors must be listed in the acknowledgements section.
