ABOUT

RNTU Journals

Rabindranath Tagore University Journals (RNTUJ) is the official academic research publishing platform of Rabindranath Tagore University established to encourage research-driven education, innovation, and scholarly contribution across multiple disciplines. The platform publishes peer-reviewed biannual journals that provide researchers, scholars, academicians, scientists, industry professionals, and students an opportunity to share impactful research with the global academic community.

RNTUJ focuses on creating a strong research culture by supporting quality publications, interdisciplinary studies, innovation, and knowledge exchange through accessible online journals.

Why Researchers Trust Us

RNTU Journals provides researchers, academicians, scholars, industry professionals, and students a trusted peer-reviewed platform to publish innovative and impactful research across multidisciplinary domains. With a strong focus on academic excellence, research integrity, and global knowledge sharing, the journal promotes high-quality publications that contribute to scientific advancement, technological innovation, social development, and interdisciplinary learning.

Through its open-access publishing model, expert editorial support, transparent review process, and wide research visibility, RNTUJ helps authors reach a broader academic audience while encouraging meaningful research collaborations and intellectual growth.
